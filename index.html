ABOUT NOISLI

Noisli is here to make you more focused and boost your productivity. A background noise generator that helps you drown out annoying noises and that lets you create your perfect environment for working and relaxing. You can mix different sounds and create your perfect sound environment tailored to your personal need and taste.

SELF-STARTED WITH PASSION

Noisli started as a side project in September 2013. At that time me and Sabine were working as freelance designers from home. It was hard to motivate ourselves and to not feel isolated since the environment around us was too silent and not inspiring. On the other hand, working in an office or co-working space is often too loud and has too many distractions. Thatâ€™s why we decided to create our own solution to the problem, Noisli.
